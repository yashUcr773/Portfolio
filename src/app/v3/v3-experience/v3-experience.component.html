About Me

The cusp of art and technology has always fascinated me, and I've never been afraid to jump in and give it a go. Whether designing and developing mobile apps, websites, transforming websites into multi-OS desktop apps, or delving into AI models, I enjoy exploring the convergence of creativity and technology.

With over 5 years of experience in software development, my focus has been creating cross-platform, scalable, and interactive content. Throughout my career, I have honed my skills in crafting engaging user experiences that seamlessly adapt to different devices and platforms. However, I don't like to define myself solely by the work I've done. Proper growth comes from the work I aspire to do. While skills can be taught and acquired, one's personality and drive are inherent. Therefore, I strive to keep learning, continuously challenge myself, and engage in exciting projects that have a meaningful impact.

Writing code is not just a job for me; it's a passion. I am constantly immersed in the process of building something new, something that pushes the boundaries of what technology can achieve. I can conjure a working product from nothing, speak the language of 0s and 1s, and talk to the overlord Linux servers to host the product. The satisfaction I derive from seeing my ideas come to life through lines of code is immeasurable.
